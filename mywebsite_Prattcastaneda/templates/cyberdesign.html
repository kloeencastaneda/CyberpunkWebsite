Cyber Fashion
❮ ❯

There are many trends that can be observed within futuristic design, whether it be graphic design or even just illustrations. Even ads showcase certain elements of cyberpunk-esque design.

Design such as this isn't only prominent in our graphics, video games, and movies. We can also see the emergence of cyberpunk fashion, such as in China, where the cyberpunk-aesthetic is showcased much more glamorously- and less dystopian- than maybe it should. All in all, the reach of the "high-tech, low-life" design/aesthetic doesn't stop at media. {%endblock%}
